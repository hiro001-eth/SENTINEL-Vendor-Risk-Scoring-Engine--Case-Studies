# Case Study: Scaling Vendor Risk Assessments for High-Growth Financial Technology

## Executive Summary
A rapidly scaling financial technology provider faced a critical bottleneck in their third-party risk management program. Their manual spreadsheet-based assessment process could not keep pace with their vendor onboarding requirements. By implementing SENTINEL, they automated their entire ingestion pipeline, reducing assessment turnaround time by 85 percent while increasing assessment accuracy.

## The Challenge
The organization was onboarding over 100 new vendors quarterly. Their existing process involved distributing Excel questionnaires, manually reviewing responses, and subjectively scoring vendors. This led to:
* Inconsistent risk scoring across different analysts.
* Inability to process high volumes of vendors efficiently.
* Lack of real-time visibility into the overall vendor risk portfolio.

## The SENTINEL Solution
SENTINEL was deployed to replace the manual workflows with a deterministic, API-driven ingestion engine. 

### Automated Ingestion and Validation
The team utilized SENTINEL's bulk CSV upload capabilities. Analysts now upload standardized response data directly into the engine, which automatically validates the data schema and processes the scoring in milliseconds.

### Real-Time Telemetry
Once processed, the executive dashboard immediately updates, providing a consolidated view of the entire vendor ecosystem. The automated pipeline categorizes vendors into strict risk tiers without human bias.

## Business Impact
* **Efficiency Gains:** Reduced average vendor assessment time from 4 hours to under 10 seconds per vendor.
* **Scalability:** The organization successfully processed 500 vendors in a single quarter without adding additional headcount to the GRC team.
* **Accuracy:** Eliminated scoring discrepancies by relying entirely on SENTINEL's deterministic YAML rule engine.
